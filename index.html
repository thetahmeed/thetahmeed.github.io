About Me

Turning Ideas into
Impactful Apps

I’m Md. Tahmeedul Islam, a passionate mobile app developer, problem solver, and innovator with 4+ years of experience building cross-platform apps using Flutter. From Android and iOS to full-fledged web applications, I specialize in creating seamless, scalable, and user-friendly digital experiences. Currently working as a Software Engineer at Guideasy Global Ltd, I’ve led and contributed to real-world products like Guideasy, Guideasy Guider, and AmarGameShop. Beyond development, I’ve competed in prestigious tech competitions like the ICPC Asia Dhaka Regional, DUET IUPC, and the NASA Space Apps Challenge, where innovation and teamwork are key. Whether you need a polished app, a smart backend, or technical guidance — I’m here to bring your ideas to life.
